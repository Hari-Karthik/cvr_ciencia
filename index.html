About us

College

CVR College of Engineering, established in 2001, recently celebrated it's 20th anniversary. The vision of the institution is: To be a state of the art institution of engineering in pursuit of excellence, in the service of society.

CIENCIA

CIENCIA is the fusion of both technical and non-technical fests which gives the platform for students to showcase their knowledge and creativity in an amusing way. It's popularity has skyrocketed in recent years as it continues to push the frontiers of inventiveness. The degree of diversity in the events is what attracts such a wide audience.
